About Us

What is GlasgowGigs?

GlasgowGigs is a web app that connects music fans with local music events in Glasgow. Using GlasgowGigs, users can find all the upcoming gigs in Glasgow, as well as popular venues and artists in Glasgow. On GlasgowGigs you will find pages for Artists, Venues and Events. These pages contain information about the artist/venue/event. On the artist page, you will find information on the artist as well as the artist's Instagram, SoundCloud, Twitter etc. On the venue page, there is information about the location, including its whereabouts on GoogleMaps. On the Events page information such as the time, venue and artist of the event is displayed.

Through our login feature on GlasgowGigs, users are able to save events that they are interested in, as well as bookmark booked events. Also, users can save their favorite artists and venues for the purpose of future gigs and events. GlasgowGigs is a accessible platform for music fans to keep track of all of the music events that they are interested in.

Future Plans for GlasgowGigs

We plan on expanding GlasgowGigs to the whole of Scotland. Our aim is to let artists have control over where and how they play their gigs, as well as edit their own pages and information to reach out to their fans. We also aim to let venues edit their own pages in terms of recent photos as well as information.

Who are we?

This website was created by Daniela, Martina and Julia. We are three 2nd year students studying Computer Science at the University of Glasgow. Our common interest in both Web Programming and Music formed the idea of creating GlasgowGigs as we all noticed that there is no easy platform for music fans in Glasgow to find all the upcoming gigs for local music, as well as a collective website that has information on Glaswegian artists and venues.
